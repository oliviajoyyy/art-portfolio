Reactions in Life

Net Art (2024)

Art 109 SJSU

Open project in new window

This Net Art project was inspired by the quote “Life is 10% what happens to you and 90% how you react to it” stated by Charles R. Swindoll. My project is meant to convey this idea that unexpected situations and ones that are out of our control may happen to us, but the way we react to it determines how we experience life. We can experience life in a more positive way by making the best of an unfortunate situation, rather than dwelling on the negatives. In my project, users are presented with 5 separate scenarios, shown one at a time. Each scenario has two options the user can choose from, with one being the positive reaction and the other being the negative reaction. There is a positivity indicator in the background, represented by the smiley face image. Each time the user selects the positive reaction, the smiley face image grows larger and the background color brightens. When the user selects the negative reaction, the smiley face image decreases in size and the background color darkens. With this, my project conveys the idea that with each positive reaction we have, our outlook on life gets brighter and happiness grows. The quote is finally revealed to the user at the end to encourage them to reflect on how they would react to situations in life.
