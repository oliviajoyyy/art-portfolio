Treasure Haunt

p5.js Video Game (2023)

Art 75 SJSU

Open project in new window

See sketch.js code

This was a game I designed and created using p5.js for my Art 75 final project. The objective of the game is to collect certain treasures throughout the house. Use the W, A, S, D keys to control the ghost character and move between rooms by heading toward the green squares. There are 5 levels. As the the levels progress, there are more items to collect and spiders to avoid. After running into a spider three times during a level, you'll have to try again. I created this game in the way that the ghost can move between rooms because I wanted to give players the freedom to explore.
