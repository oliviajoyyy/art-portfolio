Olivia Joy Cacdac


Bio / Artist statement:

I am an undergraduate student at San Jose State University majoring in Computer Science, with a minor in Digital Media Art. My work in digial media art is driven by code, specifically JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, with the goal of providing interactive experiences. I have created net art, drawing tools, video games, and more to invite viewers to feel connected and immersed in the art.
